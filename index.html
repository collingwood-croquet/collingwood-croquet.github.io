A group of local volunteers and the Municipality of Collingwood recreational.

We play American, Association and Golf Croquet, Six Wicket, on two full-size lawns at the club. We also welcome lawn bowling to invited groups and to events.

Our club house has a range of players from introductory to advanced.

We are open and comfortable croquet facilities in the area.

During the May to October season, club members play on Mondays, Wednesdays and Saturdays, beginning at 10:00 A.M.

Playing times will be published.
